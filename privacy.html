At TechPulse, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

We may collect the following types of information:

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
